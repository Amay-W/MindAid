About MindAid

MindAid is an innovative platform designed to bridge the gap in mental health support for students and counselors, providing a seamless, technology-driven solution.

Why MindAid?

With the increasing demand for mental health services, there is a critical need for platforms that cater to both students and mental health professionals. MindAid is designed to streamline appointment scheduling, session tracking, and resource sharing, addressing the unique challenges faced by counselors and students alike.

Our goal is to make mental health management accessible and efficient, empowering counselors to provide better care and helping students navigate their mental health journey with confidence.
